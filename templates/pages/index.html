  About Tahtalı Karadut Village

 Tahtalıkaradut is a village in Musabeyli district of Kilis Province, Turkey. At 36°56′N 36°55′E it is situated in the typical peneplain area of the south eastern Turkey.
 It is 25 kilometres (16 mi) north of Musabeyli and 58 kilometres (36 mi) north of Kilis.
 The population of Tahtalıkaradut is 1353 [1] as of 2010. The main products of the village are olive, grapes and cereals.
