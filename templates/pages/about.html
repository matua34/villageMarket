Tahtalıkaradut is a village in Musabeyli district of Kilis Province, Turkey.
 At 36°56′N 36°55′E it is situated in the typical peneplain area of the south eastern Turkey.
 It is 25 kilometres north of Musabeyli and 58 kilometres north of Kilis. The population of Tahtalıkaradut is 2000 as of 2020


 The main products of the village are olive, grapes and cereals.
